What is lorem ipsum?

Lorem ipsum is dummy text, derived from Latin literature, and typically starts with "Lorem ipsum dolor sit amet..". It is frequently used as placeholder text, and is used in this game to simulate what it would be like to actually have to type out a bunch of lorem ipsum text.

How to Play

A timer counting down from 1 minute will start when you start typing. Each time you finish typing a word just simply press the space bar to continue to the next word and clear the input.

When the timer runs out, you'll be provided your results measured in words per minute and accuracy.
